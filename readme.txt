== Description ==
**HeyDay Search More** boosts engagement and conversions, especially for merchants with large product inventories. It helps reduce customer abandonment by keeping users on your site and turning potential exits into sales. Ideal for optimizing the shopping experience, the app supports revenue growth by retaining customers.

### Key Features:
* **Site Search:** Real-time, responsive search for WooCommerce stores. Indexes your products to deliver fast and accurate search results.
* **Search More:** Display additional search suggestions when users attempt to leave your site. Encourages further browsing and product discovery, increasing conversions.
* **Multiple Language Support:** The search is available in English, Spanish, German, Hebrew, Armenian, Italian, Japanese, Dutch, French, Danish, and Polish.
* **Product Feed Generation:** Generates a product feed link for your WooCommerce store, enabling enhanced search functionality.
* **Seamless Setup:** Automatically registers your site and user with the HeyDay service.
* **Settings Management:** Manage the feed from the WordPress admin panel with an easy-to-use settings page.
* **Analytics Dashboard:** Redirects to the HeyDay management system for configuration, analytics, and advanced settings.

== Installation ==
1. Navigate to the 'Add New' in the plugins dashboard.
2. Search for 'HeyDay Search More'.
3. Click 'Install Now'.
4. Activate the plugin on the Plugin dashboard.
5. Manage settings in the 'HeyDay Search More' menu in the admin panel.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= How often is the product feed updated? =
The feed is updated live and hourly.

= Do I need to sign up for this service? =
No. Once you install the plugin, the necessary configurations will be set up automatically.

= Does this plugin collect personal data? =
No, HeyDay Search More does not collect or store personal data from your site visitors. The plugin interacts with external servers to manage the product feed.
